Account Setup Guide

Use this walkthrough to prepare the details our registration form requires and to understand what happens after you submit your request.

Before you begin

Gather the contact information that appears on your quotes. The review team uses these details to verify your identity and configure your user profile.

  • A business email address that you monitor daily. Shared inboxes can delay the confirmation message and password reset requests.
  • Your direct phone number and the company's main dispatch or office line so operations can reach you quickly when loads change.
  • The legal or trade name of your organization. We display this name in the header of every quote you send to customers.

Required fields explained

Each field on the registration form matches a section of the user profile. Provide accurate data to avoid delays while administrators approve access.

First and last name
Use the name that should appear in quote signatures. If you commonly go by a nickname, include it in parentheses (for example, Jonathan (Jon)).
Email address
We route confirmation emails and password resets to this address. Double-check the spelling before submitting the form to avoid lost messages.
Phone number
Enter a direct line where dispatch or accounting can reach you for urgent shipment updates. Mobile numbers are acceptable.
Company name
Provide the legal or DBA name that appears on customer-facing documents. This value populates the quote header.
Company phone
Supply the general line customers call for booking and billing questions. If your organization has multiple branches, choose the location associated with your dispatch team.
Password
Create a unique password that meets the complexity guidance shown on the form. Longer passphrases or a mix of upper-case, lower-case, numbers, and symbols improve security.
Human verification
Solve the short math prompt at the bottom of the form. This step keeps bots from creating fake accounts and alerts us to repeated failed attempts.

Confirmation emails and next steps

After you click Register, the quote tool immediately confirms the submission on screen. You'll also receive a confirmation email summarizing the details you provided. If you do not see it within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder and mark the message as safe for future notifications.

Once an administrator finishes the review, you'll get a second email that indicates whether the account is ready to use or if more information is required. Employee registrations from a @freightservices.net address are flagged for manual approval, so the follow-up email may take slightly longer.

Keep both messages for your records. They include the information you need to verify your login and confirm that your profile details are correct.
